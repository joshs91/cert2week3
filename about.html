Pokemon's Final Form!

Combining all the most basic elements of past pokemon games into one forgettable javascript web game! Use your wits and cunning in our procedurally generated battles, which allow limitless creativity and freedom, with the players own choice from one of two possible attacks, and the risk/reward based stamina regen aand health regen moves! Fight how YOU want to, with our intuitive controls, you will find moving around our beautiful map effortless as you use the exact same keys you've used countless times before in video games. Pokemon: Uranium is a once in a lifetime game, utilizing innovation and the critical programming skills taught by the master himself, Big Benny Boy, all hail, best teacher 2016.

Controls:
WASD: Movement

Attacks:
w: slash
s: pound
a: stamina regen
d: health regen
